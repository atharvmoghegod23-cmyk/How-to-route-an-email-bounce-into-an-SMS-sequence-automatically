Project Summary

A complete overview of the email bounce to SMS automation platform and its next evolution.

Recap

This project demonstrates how to route email bounces into SMS sequences automatically. It covers:

  • Why: Preserving lead velocity, bypassing inbox friction, and improving operational efficiency.
  • How: Step-by-step architecture from event detection to SMS dispatch.
  • Code: Real Node.js examples for webhook endpoints, routing logic, and SMS gateway integration.
  • Compliance: Ensuring opt-in and regulatory adherence before sending SMS.

Key Takeaways

Automation ensures that communication failures don't mean lost opportunities. By combining ESP webhooks with SMS gateways, you create a resilient, cross-channel engagement pipeline.

Next Steps

Future enhancements could include:

  • Adding retry logic with exponential backoff for soft bounces.
  • Integrating analytics dashboards to track SMS conversion rates.
  • Expanding to WhatsApp or push notifications for multi-channel coverage.
